Setting, Dose, Set & Technique.

What you will experiance through DMT cannot be explained or predicted. For the most profound effects, it is generally advised to prepare a ceremonial setting. Below, we have created a short guideline on how to safely and effectively use DMT. The following is in order of preparation. For example, I find it best to prepare the doses before starting the ceremony, as even simple tasks can snap you out of the meditative state (set).

 
 
1
 
 

1. Setting

Meaning to prepare your environment for the experience. Switch off any artificial lights, light a few candles, burn some incense and play a suitable meditation track. Sharp or interrupted sounds can snap you out of a trip prematurely, so we prefer to choose something ambient. “Silent darkness” is also a great option if you already practise meditation.

2. Dose

This varies slightly from person to person, but I’ve found 50mg (0.05g) of freebase DMT to be a reliable dose if the vaping technique is good (see below).

3. Set

Meaning to ensure you're in a calm place mentally before taking the dose. Proper setting, as above, is an important factor. Ensure you’re sitting comfortably and meditate until your mind is clear and you feel completely relaxed before proceeding, this can take a while, don't rush. Pre-trip anxiety is perfectly normal.

4. Technique

Meaning vaping technique. You should inhale the full dose in as few tokes as possible, ideally within three. This is to ensure you’re capable of inhaling the full dose before the DMT takes full effect. Don't rush though, it’s important to maintain your meditative state (set). Hold the vapour down deep in your lungs for a good 10 seconds, exhale, then go straight back for another deep toke, and repeat until the full dose is used. Try not to wait between tokes, and don’t surrender to the inevitable “I’ve had enough!” feeling, this is just a survival instinct of the ego.
